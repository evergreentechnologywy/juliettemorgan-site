About Juliette

Behind the Beauty

Based in Paris and New York, Juliette Morgan is an international fashion model known for her strong editorial presence, unique versatility, and captivating runway stride. Represented by leading global agencies, she has worked with prestigious luxury design houses, renowned cosmetics brands, and top-tier editorial publications.

Known for her high professionalism, artistic expression, and collaborative spirit, Juliette continues to expand her boundaries across campaigns, runway shows, and artistic installations.
